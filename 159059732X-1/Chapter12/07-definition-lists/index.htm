SLR
Abbreviation of Single Lens Reflex
A specific type of camera - one that uses a mirror to display the exact image to be capture through the viewfinder
SLR cameras are usually used by professional, semi-professional and hobbyists as they offer greater creative control than a point-and-shoot camera
